2014 ITP Code Poetry Slam

The 2014 ITP Code Poetry Slam was an enormous success, thanks mostly to the incredible judges and presenters.

The judges brought a wealth of experience from a variety of different fields, which provided for some extraordinary discussion. They were:

  • Bob Holman
  • Nick Montfort
  • Allison Parrish
  • Daniel Shiffman (in absentia)
